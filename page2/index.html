2. ANU Wattle

Wattle is a Moodle site ANU uses: to provide online delivery of course material (echo360); for laboratory (CLAB and HLAB) and tutorial (TUTE) group selection; for submission of assessment tasks; to enable participation in discussions (forums), provide gradebook feedback and support collaboration. RAK Projects Geriatric Ramblings uses Wattle for course administration.

This website is used for: course content; handouts for laboratories and tutorials, downloads for course lecture slides; downloads for course lecture notes/bricks; additional resources and links; updates on materials, etc.
