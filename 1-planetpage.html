 Planet Astrophotography

Here we look at the hardware setup for high resolution imaging of the planets Jupiter, Saturn and Mars.

Design Guidelines

  • The physical length of camera and opticals as short as possible
  • Spacing for the Powermate should be as close as possible to recommended distance to sensor (56mm)

Key Elements

  • You need big aperture (20cm +) and long focal length (Cassegrain/SCT and a Barlow/Powermate).
  • Collimate your telescope; then collimate again because it has to be perfect.
  • With a SCT, lock down your primary focus (which moves the main mirror); if you change this setting then you’ll probably need to repeat collimation.
  • You don’t need good off-axis optical performance – a standard SCT will perform as well as an EdgeHD or ACF.
  • As the high magnification required the atmospheric dispersion can be significant so shoot the planet away from the horizon; ideally around the meridian. Atmospheric dispersion correctors do work but are fiddly to adjust and need continupus adjustment as the planet changes altitude.

Components (in order from telescope towards camera)

  1. GSO Crayford Focuser for SCT 10:1 (2 Inch) – M-SCT001
  • to enable motorized focusing
  • 1.25” adapter is included and used with the above focuser
  1. Tele Vue Powermate 2.5x – PMT-2513
    • one of the most highly regarded image magnifiers
  2. Tele Vue 1.25” Powermate T-Ring Adapter – PTR-1250
    • Camera-side T-ring shoulder is the optical reference point 0mm
    • Camera-side thread: T2 male (M42 x 0.75)
    • Optical length: 0mm
  3. TS Optics T2 quick changer and 360° rotator – TST2Rot
    • Enables alignment of camera axes (with telescope axes)
    • Telescope-side thread: T2 female (M42 x 0.75)
    • Camera-side thread: T2 male (M42 x 0.75)
    • Optical length (w/o T2 male thread): 5.5mm
  4. ZWO 21mm T2 to T2 Extension T-Ring Spacer – SKU: ZWO-EFW-8x1.25
    • Spacer to get near the correct backfocus for the Powermate (T-Ring Adapter shoulder to the sensor)
    • Telescope-side thread: T2 female (M42 x 0.75)
    • Camera-side thread: T2 male (M42 x 0.75)
    • Optical length (w/o T2 male thread): 21mm
  5. ZWO EFW Filter Wheel 8×1.25” – SKU: ZWO-EFW-8x1.25
    • Sses the new ZWO LRGB filter set
    • Telescope-side thread: T2 female (M42 x 0.75)
    • Camera-side thread: T2 female (M42 x 0.75)
    • Optical length: 20mm
  6. ZWO Anti-dew heater strip (not used; legacy from DSO setup) – SKU: ZWO-Anti-dew
    • Optical length: ~1mm
  7. ZWO ASI183MM Pro (cooling not used; legacy from DSO setup)) – SKU: ASI183MM-P
    • Telescope-side thread: T2 male (M42 x 0.75)
    • Optical length (w/o T2 male thread and to sensor): 6.5mm
